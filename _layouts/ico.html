PROTECTING YOUR INVESTMENT.

Introduction of Reserve and
Escrow Agents

Your investment is protected by our 3of4 multi-signature of escrow agents. The raised investment will remain fully auditable in Reserve of Nutz contract After Crowdsale. The Ether raised will be payed out in milestones one after the other as above and the first milestone will be payed out when the crowdsale ends. IN IT FOR THE LONG RUN.

Power Token Distribution

We only sell 10-30% of the total Power in this crowdsale, showing our commitment to the long-term success of the project. We envision an economy controlled by its stakeholders through voting, hence requiring a high degree of smart money. The Acebusters economy has the ability to execute further growth events in which we issue more Power (dilluting existing shares) and collect more capital to expand to new markets and build better products. We are commited to a sustainable growth path, selling power not all at once, but repeatedly, achieving the best price and finding high quality investors. We are prepared to give up our majority share when the Dapp has grown to sustainable on-chain governance by it’s stakeholders.
