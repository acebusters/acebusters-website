Currency for games, bets and bankroll.

PROTECTING YOUR INVESTMENT. Introduction of Reserve and
Escrow Agents

Your investment is protected by our 3of4 multi-signature of escrow agents. The raised investment will remain fully auditable in Reserve of Nutz contract After Crowdsale. The Ether raised will be payed out in milestones one after the other as above and the first milestone will be payed out when the crowdsale ends. So without an independent party signing, we will not be able to withdraw funds.
